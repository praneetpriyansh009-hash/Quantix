OUR NORMS

Quantix Holds Itself
to a Higher Standard.

💧

Negligible Water

Quantix mandates closed-loop liquid cooling in its infrastructure. The heat leaves. The water stays.

Our engineering norm: sealed dielectric fluid systems where coolant circulates continuously, transfers heat, and returns without evaporative loss. No exceptions.

⚡

Nuclear + Renewables

Quantix's long-term infrastructure standard requires clean, stable, carbon-free power — both nuclear and renewable, together.

Renewables are essential but carry intermittency. Nuclear is carbon-free and energy-dense. Our company norm is to build around both — because responsible engineering needs power that is clean, stable, and available at scale.

🧠

Build Leaner

Quantix's efficiency standard: half the compute, same output quality. Quantization, pruning, knowledge distillation — by design, not afterthought.

Every system we ship treats computational efficiency as a primary engineering specification. Smaller compute. Lower energy. Lower water. Not less intelligent — more consciously engineered.
